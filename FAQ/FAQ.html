Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new account?

To create a new account go to the home page and click either student or teacher to sign up with your email, username, and password.

Where is the verification email for my new account?

It should be in your inbox or spam folder, if it is not in your inbox or your spam folder, you request a new login email on the login page.

What if I forgot my password?

If you forgot your password you can recover your account and create a new password by clicking the "forgot password" option that will intially pop up on the login page that you are originally directed to when you begin to log in with your email address.
